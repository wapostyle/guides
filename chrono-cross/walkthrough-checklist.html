00) Dream Sequence
01) Arni Village [Home World]
He'll also mention some information about Poshul.
A save point (Record of Fate) is here but occupied by townsfolk early on.
Poshul (Innate: Yellow): Found outside Radius' house. Show her the Heckran Bone key item to recruit her. It's recommended to get her now, though she can still be found later if missed here.
Optional: Cape Howl, northwest of Arni, has Beach Bum enemies and water jets dealing -5 HP. It contains a Heal element and Bone components only.
Before leaving, consider swapping any Tablets for a healing element and equipping Poshul with Uplifts.
02) Lizard Rock [Home World]
Fight as many battles here as possible for small stat increases before facing the area boss. Some battles may give nothing before better ones appear.
Don't waste your good elements on the minions leading up to this fight; save them for the boss.
BOSS: Mama Komodo
Innate: Blue   HP: 160   Spoils: @Fang, Tablet
Elements: AquaBeam
Specials: Breath (blue-innate damage to all allies); SquirtGun (blue-innate damage to single target)
Pattern: Attack to AquaBeam/Attack to SquirtGun to Breath (×??)
DIALOGUE CHOICE: Opassa Beach scene with Leena
Answer that Serge does "remember the day", then follow up with "we'll never forget this day!!". These responses count toward eventually unlocking Leena's "MaidenFaith" tech.
03) Lizard Rock [Another World]
Optional: Cape Howl: a chest holds an ElectroJolt element, and a @Bone container sits above where the Beach Bums used to be.
Optional: Hydra Marshes: walking through the water drains 2 HP per step. Collect a Tablet, a Bushwhacker element, and an ElectroJolt element across the screens. A man at the western dead end gives the Safety Gear key item, which removes the marsh's damage.
