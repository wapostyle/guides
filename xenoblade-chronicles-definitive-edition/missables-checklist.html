This checklist is organized by story cutoff point, in the order they occur. Complete everything listed under a cutoff before triggering that cutoff's boss or event. One convention used throughout: many weapon and armor appearances ("fashion" skins) are missable but unlock permanently to your game data rather than to a specific save file — so you can save, buy or obtain the item, then reload your save and keep the unlocked appearance. This applies to every shop and drop skin noted below.
Cutoff 1 — Refugees Move to Colony 6
Triggered by completing the quest "The Road Home" (available after Satorl Marsh). This cutoff happens at a point of your choosing, not automatically — Juju gives you a clear warning before the refugees relocate.
"The Road Home" is mutually exclusive with the later quest "To Colony 6!". If you skip "The Road Home" before meeting Miqol in Hidden Machina Village, Dulland offers the substitute "To Colony 6!" instead (better EXP/gold reward, but it starts Colony 6 reconstruction all the same).
Refugee Camp Quests
These NPCs move to Colony 6 once reconstruction begins. Missable quests are marked with a stopwatch icon in your quest log. A few Refugee Camp quests are not missable — such as Earnest's — because he does not relocate.

Cutoff 2 — Jade Face (End of Mechonis Field)
Fighting the boss Jade Face at the end of Mechonis Field permanently locks Sword Valley and Galahad Fortress. Finish everything in both zones first.

Cutoff 3 — Meyneth Shrine (Agniratha)
The boss at the Meyneth Shrine at the end of Agniratha locks Mechonis Field (including Machina Refuge), Agniratha, and the Hidden Machina Village quests that require Mechonis Field access.

Agniratha — Terminal Quests (14)
Once all 14 are complete, interact with the last terminal at Central Tower to receive the Cloister Key, which unlocks the door on 3F leading to a secret area.

Other Missables
The cutoffs above capture most of the game's major missable content. A few extras don't tie to a single cutoff point.
Missable Achievements
🏆 Heartwarming — Have a Heart-to-Heart go as smoothly as possible.
🏆 Heartbreaking — Have a Heart-to-Heart go as badly as possible.
🏆 The Brave Protectors — Get to know all of the Defence Force soldiers in Colony 9.
Heartwarming and Heartbreaking are missable because there are only a limited number of Heart-to-Hearts (63 total) — you could clear all of them without ever picking the best or worst option. The Brave Protectors appears to be a technical quirk: speak with Andreas, Dorothy, Kantz, Emmy Leater, Miller, Minnie, Monica, Nic, and Raoul in Colony 9. It may become impossible if you invite Nic to Colony 6 before talking with all of them.
