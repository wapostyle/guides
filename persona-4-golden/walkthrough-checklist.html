April 27 — Yukiko's Castle (Dungeon 1)
Today is a rainy day. Complete the rainy-only purchases below before entering the TV World.
🏆 Seize the Moment (5/49) — Buy the Super Croquette on a rainy day
Persona stock for this dungeon (by the final boss): Strength, Chariot, Justice, Hierophant, Sun, Magician, and Temperance arcana (e.g. Valkyrie, Eligor, Archangel, Omoikane, Cu Sith, Orobas, Apsaras). You can only hold 7+ once you hit level 15.
🏆 Grasping at Greed (6/49) — Defeat a Golden Hand
Optional farming: keep a Gentlemen's Tux (locked chest, 3F–4F / 6F–7F) and Golden Cloths (Phantom Mage, 4F–6F) for later quests. Golden Dishes (Heat Balance, 6F–7F) are only for an optional Artisian Apprentice cosmetic weapon.
🏆 The Nose Doesn't Always Know (7/49) — Trigger a fusion accident
🏆 Persona Shopper (8/49) — Buy a Persona from the Compendium
🏆 A Favour for Marie (9/49) — Register a Skill Card
Reach level 15 with ~25,000 yen (30,000 on Very Hard) and at least one gemstone before the 8F boss.
At 50% HP she summons a Prince (silence / fear / heals her) that flees once hurt enough. Main danger is Burn to Ashes (fire AoE): guard everyone the turn after she "passionately stares," and late in the fight she stops telegraphing it — count turns, it lands when her White Wall expires. Terror Voice + Shivering Rondo can one-shot a feared ally.

May 22 — Steamy Bathhouse (Dungeon 2)
Buy Tomato Seedlings from the Housewife and plant them — planting and harvesting cost no time and yield SP items; harvest within a couple of days before they wilt.
Pet the Fox on his good-mood days for hidden costumes (the first one needs two pets across good-mood days).
Save your Ice Cubes for the Bathhouse's Golden Hands — don't spend them on Yukiko's Castle Golden Hands.
If you still need the Gentlemen's Tux, farm locked chests on 3F–4F (rng — save before farming keys). This is your second-to-last shot before the quest needs it.
🏆 Skilled Commander (12/49) — Perform 50 All-Out Attacks
🏆 Tactical Fighter (13/49) — Exploit enemy weaknesses 100 times
Persona stock for the boss — Priestess, Jester, Magician, Chariot, Sun, Hierophant, Justice, Moon, Temperance arcana (e.g. Gurr, Anzu, Principality, Andra, Phoenix, Jack Frost, plus Saki Mitama, Eligor, Xiezhai). Reach level 25 before the boss to hold 10 Personas.
Optional boss: skip the Contrarian King (atop Yukiko's Castle) this visit — its Rampage can one-shot the party on Hard/Very Hard. It's covered next month.
Reach level 25 with ~85,000 yen (after selling loot) before the 11F boss.
He drains Wind — don't use Yosuke's Wind skills, they heal him. Two adds join him: kill Tough Guy (weak Fire) and Nice Guy (weak Ice) first. Guard everyone on Power Charge, and watch for Fanatical Spark (party-wide Electric at HP thresholds) which KOs an unguarding Yosuke; Roar of Wrath enrages the girls — cure it with Me Patra.
