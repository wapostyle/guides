Nanai (Night Only)
Nanai can only be entered at night. The day/night cycle advances with steps taken on the overworld — walk around until it turns night if needed.
Do NOT walk right of the Dragon Shrine — the guard will catch you and block your progress.

Red mushrooms in this forest restore HP. Yellow mushrooms are poisonous — avoid them.
