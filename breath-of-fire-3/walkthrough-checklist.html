1. Hoodlums in the Woods
Dauna Mine
Cedar Woods and McNeil
Yraall Road and Bunyan's House
Swing the axe when the log tips — the timing window is generous and you should win on your first attempt.
Road to Mt. Glaus
Mt. Glaus and the Caverns
Boss: NUE (HP 285, recommended lv 6) — Chlorine poisons and Jolt hits all for 10–15 damage — Rei can Pilfer Power Food twice: once when the Nue runs away during the first fight, and again in this final fight — Simoon from Teepo (lv 7+) shortens this fight significantly
Yraall Region (Spring)
Outside McNeil Manor
Have a solid supply of healing items before heading to the manor — there are several mandatory ghost fights inside and no shop access — also bring at least 50z to pay off a guard on the grounds
Avoid all lantern-carrying guards until after ringing the bell — if one spots you, you are ejected from the grounds and must pay the initial guard another 50z to re-enter
Inside McNeil Manor
Torast uses Sleep frequently. If you have the Silver Knife, equip it — it deals major damage to all ghosts in this manor.
McNeil Manor Roof
Boss: AMALGAM (HP 520, recommended lv 9) — formed from all the McNeil ancestor ghosts — Silver Knife deals major damage — watch for Astral Warp (15–20 to one character) and occasional Sleep
Aftermath
Balio and Sunder attack at the Cedar Woods house — this fight cannot be won — do not waste healing items; Rei can use Pilfer once or twice if you want extra items before going down
2. The Balio and Sunder Saga
Mt. Myrneg
Wyndian Dungeon
Dragon Gene: FLAME — obtained automatically after breaking out of the dungeon cell.
Boss: BALIO AND SUNDER — this fight does not need to be won — use a Healing Herb on Nina first so she can escape — then use Accession with the Flame gene (Flame Whelp) and use Flame Breath; or cast Influence on Balio so Sunder attacks him instead — winning earns 110 exp but is not required
Wyndian Catacombs
Wyndia
The four hiding kids are: behind trees near the Inoculation shop, behind the left wall near the city entrance, behind the weapon shop near the bum, and behind the tree in the eastern café area.
Eygnock Road and Genmel
Mt. Boumore
Dragon Gene: DEFENDER — received automatically after Ryu transforms to break Nina's fall.
Momo's Tower
Stock up on healing items before entering the Tower — no shops inside and several floors to climb
Dragon Gene: THORN — look near the trees next to the crash landing site before exiting to the world map.
Coffee Shop and Eastern Wyndia
New Master: D'LONZO — the tough-looking girl on the hill above the Coffee Shop — requires 15 different weapons in your inventory (not counting equipped weapons) to take you on as an apprentice.
The Plant and the Dump
Boss: MUTANT (HP 500, recommended lv 13) — extremely weak to fire; fire attacks also destroy its 250 HP/turn regeneration — use Momo's Flame Chrysm and Ryu's Flame dragon form throughout; keep the fire pressure on until it falls
With Peco in party, return to the [?] forest north of the Coffee Shop — have Peco kick the rocks to find a Hawk's Ring and a Vitamin in holes underneath.
Contest of Champions
When asked to leave a hostage, choose Peco — keep your stronger fighters free for the tournament
Maekyss Brawl
Get the Flower Jewel BEFORE entering Maekyss Gorge — put Peco in your party and find the [?] area west of the gorge entrance — have Peco kick the tree outside the tent for the Flower Jewel — check the tent drawers for a Wisdom Fruit — then switch Peco OUT of your party — DO NOT sell the Flower Jewel; it is needed for the Meryleep master quest
Wyndia Castle
3. Journey to Angel Tower
Rhapala Region
New Master: FAHL — return to the Arena in Genmel and speak to the barkeep — fight 30 battles in a row without resting to qualify (running away from battles counts) — great stat gains for Peco and Garr.
Port Town of Rhapala
Quick method: learn Super Combo from Bunyan first — one session where Ryu lands a Super Combo ends training. Then kill Zig in a single hit with a Super Combo (250+ damage, 8+ hits required) — must be a one-hit kill or the fight is called off.
The Lighthouse
Buy at least 1–2 extra Flame Chrysm from Rhapala shops before entering — you sacrifice one per attempt at the valve puzzle inside and cannot complete the dungeon without opening it
Boss: GAZER (HP 1100, recommended lv 17) — normal attack hits all party members for solid damage; Eye Beam hits one for 50+ — Flame Claw is highly effective and negates Eye Beam — Momo casts Protect on the whole party and cures Hypnotize confusion with Panacea — Garr uses regular attacks and Pyrokinesis — inflicting Blind on Gazer doubles your exp reward
The boulder only rolls if the chrysm line leading up the stairs is lit. If it isn't, go back to the first blue switch (the one before the valve room in c84) and hit it.
Faerie Village
Boss: DOLPHIN (HP 1200, recommended lv 17) — use Thunder+Defender dragon form for Thunder Claw (300+ damage per hit) — Tsunami cuts everyone's HP in half; Intimidate cancels your turn — Momo casts Protect on herself and Ryu; Nina heals or casts Lightning
Two other rocks in this forest cover holes with a Vitamin and a Hawk's Ring — have Peco kick them too.
Mt. Zublo
Yellow geysers inside the volcano cause confusion and reverse your D-pad controls — cure with Panacea immediately; stepping on lava deals damage but HP will never drop below 1
Urkan Tapa
Angel Tower
Keep Garr in your party — his push ability is required for the block puzzles inside the tower
4. Revenge
Dauna Mine
Come back here in Chapter 5 with Momo — blast the weak wall section nearby with her gun to find the Coupons accessory.
Boss: DRAGON ZOMBIE (HP 2500, recommended lv 21) — Rotten Breath poisons both party members; Bone Dance damages and confuses all — Ryu: cast Shield, then Flame+Eldritch dragon → Restore spell deals 500+ damage (needs decent AP and Intelligence), or Flame+Defender for Flame Claw (~300 damage) — Garr: Pyrokinesis and heals status changes with Antidotes and Vitamins — Garr's Kyrie at lv 26+ one-shots this boss
Dauna Hills Region
Keep all the Horseradish — it is a required ingredient for the Shisu recipe needed in Chapter 7.
Mt. Levett
Reunion in the Yraall Region
The Massacre
Boss: MIKBA (HP 2400, recommended lv 21) — Venom Breath poisons all; Multistrike hits multiple times in one turn; very high reprisal rate with frequent Lucky Strikes — Rei starts unconscious — Ryu: cast Shield then Flame+Defender dragon → Flame Claw — Garr: keep everyone alive with Antidotes and Vitamins — after dealing ~1000 damage, revive Rei — Weretiger Rei can go berserk and attack allies; cast Influence on Mikba first to keep Weretiger focused on him — Weretiger deals 300+ damage per hit
5. Problems at the Plant
Reunion at the Plant
Now that Momo is back, you can return to Dauna Mine B2 and blast the weak wall in the northeast room (where the MultiVitamin was) to find the Coupons accessory.
The Underground Laboratory
Learn Flame Strike from the Fly Men enemies in the lab — give it to Rei for the upcoming boss fight.
Boss: HUGE SLUG (HP 2500, recommended lv 22) — immune to physical attacks until a fire attack destroys the protective coating (a single Molotov works if no fire spells are available) — Syphon drains HP — Ryu: cast Shield first, then Force+Flame dragon → Flame Claw throughout — Momo: Flame Chrysm attacks plus Speed and Might assists
Boss: SHROOM / PALET (HP 3000, recommended lv 23) — Ragnarok randomly deals massive or zero damage to all; Head Cracker and Blitz are also dangerous — Ryu: cast Shield at least 3 times first, then Force+Defender/Thorn Warrior dragon → use Focus before each Aura attack — Rei: Pilfer the Wisdom Fruit as the first priority, then normal attacks or Weretiger (use Influence if available) — Momo: Speed and Might on Ryu and Rei, heal throughout
The Escape from Wyndia
6. Freeing Deis
Angel Tower and Urkan Tapa
Tidal Caves
The water level in the Tidal Caves rises and falls every few minutes — if a ladder is submerged, go back inside the cave and wait for the water to drop
Cliff
Boss: GAIST (HP 2500, recommended lv 25, solo Ryu) — Sanctuary erases all assist magic so skip buff casting — Howling causes confusion; Corona is a powerful fire spell — transform immediately using Force+Frost+Gross genes — destroy the two torches first (each has ~300 HP and heals Gaist with Flare every turn) or ignore them and focus Gaist directly (torches also grant Ryu extra turns) — use Frost Claw once the torches are gone; if knocked out just transform again
Help From Deis
Saying "I like you this way" is required to unlock Deis as a master later in the game.
7. To Cross the Sea
Rhapala & Junk Town
Northern Urkan Region
Fixing the Ship
Boss: ANGLER (HP 3,000, recommended lv 26) — equip Thunder Ring on Rei or Garr and bring Antidotes (uses Venom Breath to poison and Thunder Clap for single-target electric damage) — Rei casts Speed on Ryu at the start — Garr attacks with the Beast Spear — transform Ryu using Force+Thorn+Gross genes — use Focus twice then Aura for massive damage
Parch & The Shisu Quest
Return to the shisu lady at Maekyss Gorge with all 4 ingredients to get the full recipe — then go back to Parch and have the mayor's advisor help make it — optimal ratio 10:8:2:4 (Mackerel : Shally Seeds : Vinegar : Horseradish) — highest quality shisu rewards a Shaman's Ring from the advisor
Legendary Mariner
Return to Kukuys's house later to collect Protein from the drawer inside
The Black Ship
Optional masters now available — Bais, Lang, Lee, and Wynn (return to the Wyndia fountain; they challenge you to a worldmap Hide & Seek) — Hachio (Wyndia Castle kitchen basement via the portal used in Ch5's escape) — Deis (Mt. Zublo, only if you said "I like you this way" in Ch6) — good time to also return to Kukuys's house for the Protein
Boss: AMMONITES ×2 (HP 2,000 each, recommended lv 27) — high attack power with Multistrike, Tsunami, Blizzard, and Hypnotize — open with Ryu casting Shield 3-4 times (Chain formation), Rei casting Speed on everyone, Momo casting Might or War Shout — transform Ryu with Force+Thunder+Gross genes — use Focus twice then Thunder Claw to burst down one Ammonite — have Rei steal Moon Tears — once one is down, switch Rei to Weretiger to finish — Chapter 8 begins at the Lost Shore

9. Dragnier
Arriving at Dragnier
Meeting the Elder
Boss: ELDER (HP 5,000, recommended lv 30) — no dragon transformations allowed in this fight — uses Inferno, Myollnir, Sirocco, and Double Blow (very high attack power) — open with Ryu casting Shield 3-4 times and Barrier on everyone — Rei casts Speed on anyone without an extra turn — Momo casts Might or War Shout — Rei uses Weretiger and becomes the primary damage dealer (700-1,000 per hit with full buffs) — Momo and Ryu focus on healing and attacking with their best skills when possible
Optional: return to the area above the portal room and have Ryu examine the blank wall frame — Ladon appears as a master (final master in the game, teaches Aura — one of the strongest attacks available)
The Factory
10. The Desert of Death
Base Camp
Desert navigation shortcut: from the start, face between the true north star and the fake north star (1 turn right from north) and walk in that direction until you reach the Oasis — drink water when prompted (~twice per night, take a few steps then use one helping) — make camp at daybreak and break camp at nightfall — after every random battle, re-check the stars and re-orient before walking again (random battles can leave you facing the wrong direction) — if lost and out of water, make camp and talk to the rakda to return to base camp
Crossing the Desert
Boss: MANMO (HP 3,200, recommended lv 30) — high reprisal rate; also uses Quake — transform Ryu using Infinity+Trance+Radiance genes (Kaiser form) — Rei casts Speed and Momo casts Might on Ryu — use Shadowwalk or Aura in Kaiser form — Rei uses Weretiger — Momo heals and casts more Mights — casting Focus twice then using Shadowwalk in Kaiser form, or Aura in Warrior form, may one-shot it
Nina's Heat Stroke & The Oasis
11. Myria Station
Desert Region & Caer Xhan
Worker's Area
Laboratory
Boss: CHIMERA (HP 7,500, recommended lv 35) — Paralyzer causes paralysis — if all three party members are paralyzed it is game over, so keep Rei available to cure — also uses Inferno and Blizzard — open with Ryu casting Shield, Rei casting Speed, Momo casting Might or War Shout — transform Ryu to Kaiser (Infinity+Trance+Radiance) and use Shadowwalk or Triple Blow (if low on AP, use Force+Gross genes and Aura instead) — keep Rei in human form until paralysis is dealt with — Momo heals and casts Myollnir when possible
12. Eden
Path to Eden
Ryu's Dream
Ryu is alone in the dream maze — random battles are solo Ryu only — listen closely to all party member conversations
Boss: ARWAN (HP 2,400, solo Ryu, recommended lv 38) — uses Blizzard, Chill (frost damage), and Sleep — equip the Dream Ring — use the most powerful holy property attacks you have (Aura in Warrior form deals over 2,000 but prefer any less AP-costly holy attacks if available) — if you have a Holy Avenger, equip it and use Shadowwalk to deal heavy damage without dragon form and save AP — conserve as much AP as possible, you will need it for the fight immediately after
Dragon Lord
Boss: DRAGON LORD / TEEPO (HP 12,500, recommended lv 38) — equip Balance Rings on all party members for the early turns before Ryu transforms (Howling causes mass confusion — note that Kaiser form automatically grants confusion immunity once Ryu transforms) — also uses Inferno, Blizzard, Myollnir, and Triple Blow — have Rei use Pilfer first to steal Teepo's Dragon Blade, then equip it on Ryu — Ryu uses Accession immediately after (Kaiser form Infinity+Trance+Radiance is ideal; low AP fallback: Force+Gross genes) — Rei casts Speed on everyone then uses Weretiger — Momo casts Protect, Shield, Barrier, Might, and War Shout throughout and heals as needed — Focus twice before Shadowwalk in Kaiser can deal over 5,000 in one hit — long fight, keep at it
13. The Final Confrontation
The Final Stretch
⚠ The random battle enemies in this area are extremely tough — make sure your characters are at least in the high 30s before pushing forward
Reminiscent Battle Rooms
The Long Awaited Meeting
After the ending credits, do not turn the game off — wait for the song to finish and press Start to save your cleared game data
Final Boss: MYRIA (HP 20,000, recommended lv 40) — large spell arsenal including Inferno, Blizzard, Myollnir, Sirocco, Restore, and Death (Death can instantly KO a party member) — Venom hits all party members with blindness, poison, and confusion — Evil Eye causes paralysis — Holocaust deals ~150 damage to all (used near the end of the fight) — Sanctuary can negate assist spells but she uses it rarely — transform Ryu to Kaiser (Infinity+Trance+Radiance) immediately (Shadowwalk deals 1,500-2,000 per hit) — keep Wisdom Fruits on Momo to maintain Ryu's Kaiser AP — Rei and Momo open with Speed, Protect, Shield, Might, War Shout — once buffs are up, Rei uses his strongest attack skills then Weretiger — Momo keeps everyone's HP near maximum throughout — when in a desperate situation use Moon Tears or Hourglasses — don't hold anything back, use Bonebreak, Celerity, and your most powerful skills — this is what you saved all those items for
