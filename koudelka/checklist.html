DISC 1
Opening
🏆 I'm no Angel — Defeat Werewolf and save Edward from certain death
Caretaker's Quarter
[ Exterminator I ] Win 22 consecutive battles in Caretakers Quarters without fleeing — single session
🏆 Exterminator I: Caretakers Quarters — Win 22 consecutive battles without fleeing
🏆 Monastery Map — Obtain Monastery Map
🏆 They're all of Princess Alice! — Watch (do NOT skip) optional FMV in Caretakers Quarters: 1F Study
🏆 Caretakers — Meet kind elderly couple taking care of monastery
Do NOT skip the FMV in the 1F Study room — just watch it
Kitchen
Herb Garden
🏆 It wasn't the Kind of Problem that Faith Alone Could Solve — Defeat Giant Plant and join with James
Boss – Evil Plant | Weak to Fire. Vital Hammer recommended — heals Edward on each hit. Koudelka casts Flare. When Edward deals 0 damage, one more Flare finishes it.
Backtrack — 2nd Floor
Seeker Trio
🏆 Seeker Trio — Defeat Seeker Trio
Boss – Red Idol / Blue Idol / Green Idol. Kill order: Green first (Edward attacks + Koudelka Flare), then Blue, then Red last. Do NOT use Flare on Red Idol — physical attacks only. If Red turns horizontal it will unleash a big attack — try to finish it that turn. L Mail on Edward helps.
Dungeon — Upper Floor
[ Exterminator II ] Win 22 consecutive battles in Dungeon area without fleeing — single session (spans Disc 1 and Disc 2)
🏆 Exterminator II: Dungeon — Win 22 consecutive battles without fleeing
🏆 Mummy Bride — Defeat Mummy Bride
Grind to at least Level 6 before the Doppelgangers — they are significantly harder
Boss – Mira (Mummy Bride) | Undead — cast Heal directly on her to deal damage. Equip Koudelka's J Ring first for boosted Heal damage. Two or three rounds of Heal from Koudelka and James finishes her.
Priest's Quarters
🏆 Guard's Diary — Obtain Guard's Diary
🏆 Doppelgangers — Defeat Doppelgangers
Boss – Doppelgangers. Kill D. James first — he heals the others. Then D. Edward (most physical damage). D. Koudelka last. Focus all attacks on one at a time, keep someone healing. Struggling? Raise Koudelka's PIE to reduce magic damage taken.
DISC 2
Dungeon Cell
🏆 Jailer — Defeat the Jailer
Boss – Jailer | James uses Fortify STR on Edward. Koudelka casts Flare (~450 damage). Take him down before he gets close.
There is a door locked with a Green Key and the key is RIGHT NEXT TO IT — DO NOT pick up the Green Key. It triggers an unwinnable battle.
Underground Shrine — Mad Fly
[ Exterminator III ] Win battles in Underground Shrine area without fleeing
🏆 Mad Fly — Defeat Mad Fly
🏆 Exterminator III: Underground Shrine — Win consecutive battles without fleeing
Boss – Mad Fly | HP 3,050. He moves fast — keep him away from Koudelka (he can Silence her). Buff Edward with Fortify STR and VIT first. James heals Edward throughout. Koudelka uses Flare or Level 2 spell. He has a multi-hit attack for ~700 damage total.
Treasure Room
🏆 Design on the Floor — Examine the four symbols near the stairs
Storage Rooms — Dark Young
[ Exterminator IV ] Win battles in Storage Rooms area without fleeing
🏆 Old Letter — Obtain the Old Letter (obtained after Greek chest opens — see Library section)
🏆 Dark Young — Defeat Dark Young
🏆 Exterminator IV: Storage Rooms — Win consecutive battles without fleeing
Boss – Dark Young | HP 2,900, Weak to Fire. Can silence your entire party. Flare does 600–700 at Level 2. Keep Silence off with Panaceas. James heals and cures Silence; Edward attacks; Koudelka casts Flare.
Alias Fight
🏆 Alias — Defeat Alias
Boss – Alias | HP 2,100 + Crates (1,000) + Barrels (1,000). First use one Flare to destroy both Crates and Barrels. Equip Edward with the Crossbow (already in inventory) — reload every other turn but does massive damage. High enough STR kills Alias in 2 shots. Heal Edward from gun attacks.
Backtrack — Red Key Doors & Mask
🏆 Mask — Obtain the Mask
Triangle Puzzle Room
Library — Third Statue & Greek Chest
[ Exterminator V ] Win battles in Library area without fleeing
🏆 Exterminator V: Library — Win consecutive battles without fleeing
Chimera
🏆 Chimera — Defeat Chimera
Boss – Chimera | HP 5,000, Weak to Earth. Fast and can silence your whole party. Keep Chimera away from Koudelka and James. Koudelka casts Megalith (Level 2 does ~1,700 damage). James uses Fortify STR/VIT on Edward and heals. Edward attacks with your strongest weapon.
Church Nave — Stained Glass (First Visit)
[ Exterminator VI ] Win battles in Church Nave area without fleeing
🏆 Exterminator VI: Church Nave — Win consecutive battles without fleeing
🏆 Creature's Hint I — Find the first creature clue in the Nave area
On this first visit you can only place the Red Glass Part (and Brown Glass Part if it dropped from Alias). The Green Glass Part is obtained later — do NOT leave without placing what you have.
Music Box Puzzle & Roger Bacon
Stained Glass — Complete & Greek Chest
🏆 Hymn to the Angels — Complete the Stained Glass window
🏆 Old Letter — Obtain the Old Letter from the Greek chest
Nave — Gargoyle (Flee!)
You CANNOT win this fight — FLEE immediately with Koudelka. Do not waste time attacking.
DISC 3
Underground Passage — Statues & Pendant
[ Exterminator VII ] Win battles in Inner Grounds area without fleeing
[ Exterminator VIII ] Win battles in Underground Passage area without fleeing
🏆 Exterminator VII: Inner Grounds — Win consecutive battles without fleeing
🏆 Exterminator VIII: Underground Passage — Win consecutive battles without fleeing
🏆 Pendant — Obtain the Pendant from the fountain
Koudelka is alone for most of Disc 3. Tornado at Level 2 does ~1,600 damage and is your primary attack.
Make room in your inventory for the Pendant — it is required to finish the game. Do not drop it.
🏆 Creature's Hint II — Find the second creature clue in the underground passage area
Graveyard Area — Gug
[ Exterminator IX ] Win battles in Graveyard area without fleeing
🏆 Gug — Defeat Gug
🏆 Exterminator IX: Graveyard — Win consecutive battles without fleeing
Boss – Gug | HP 5,200, Weak to Wind. Koudelka is alone — spam Tornado (Level 2 does ~1,600 damage). Your HP/VIT should be high enough to tank hits. Just keep casting.
🏆 Whispers in the Dark — Examine the dead body and learn the monastery's history
🏆 Creature's Hint III — Examine Charlotte's tombstone
Charlotte's Cell — Chairs & Cupboard
🏆 Charlotte's Wrath — Defeat the Chairs and Cupboard
🏆 You are Loved! — Witness Charlotte's scene after the fight
These are boss-level enemies, not regular encounters. Kill Cupboard first (it poisons). Then kill Chair (it silences).
Boss – Cupboard (HP 4,600) + Chair (HP 4,500). Strongest magic spell (~2,000 damage) on Cupboard first. Keep both away from Koudelka and James. Once Cupboard is dead, focus all on the Chair.
Apostle Boss
[ Exterminator X ] Win battles in Patrick's Quarters area without fleeing
🏆 Apostle — Defeat the Apostle
🏆 Exterminator X: Patrick's Quarters — Win consecutive battles without fleeing
Boss – Apostle | HP 9,400, Weak to Fire. Very fast, targets Koudelka, can Silence from range. Buff Edward's STR for 2 turns first (very high physical defense). Flare at Level 2+ does ~2,200. James heals; keep Silence off Koudelka; Edward takes ~800-damage hits.
🏆 Creature's Hint IV — Find a creature clue near the Apostle fight area
Patrick's Mansion — Acid, Bomb Prep & Scale
🏆 Creature's Hint V — Find a creature clue in Patrick's Mansion
🏆 Don't Ask a Girl Her Weight — Solve the counterweight scale puzzle
🏆 Lifedrinker — Trigger the Lifedrinker event in Patrick's area
🏆 Creature's Hint VI — Hear Roger Bacon's creature hint
Before continuing: backtrack to the Mad Fly fight area and fill the Empty Bottle at the Acid Tank. This Acid is required to make the bomb and get past Patrick's door.
🏆 Creature's Hint VII — Hear Roger's second hint after the Elaine soul reveal
Doll Puzzle & Scapegoat
🏆 Scapegoat — Defeat Scapegoat
Boss – Scapegoat | HP 9,200. Back-row strategy: Edward snipes from afar, use Panaceas for Silence. If his body moves erratically, use physical attacks. If he barely moves, use magic.
🏆 Creature's Hint VIII — Find a creature clue near the Scapegoat area

Final Boss — Elaine (Three Forms)
🏆 Bested Elaine — Defeat Elaine (Form 1)
🏆 Bested Evolved Elaine — Defeat Elaine (Form 2)
Boss – Elaine Form 1 | HP 9,000, Weak to Wind. Physical attacks do NOT hurt her — keep Edward in back row using only Fortify buffs. Stay in back row, she uses ~400-damage magic. Buff Koudelka/James INT for 2 turns, then spam Tornado (~1,600+ damage). Her magic can poison — heal as needed.
Boss – Elaine Form 2 | HP 12,000, Weak to Earth. Now physical attacks hurt more than magic. Equip Edward with the Crossbow — 2,000+ damage per shot at high STR. Third character uses Megalith. Everyone stays in back row.
Boss – Elaine Form 3 | HP 17,000. KEEP EVERYONE IN THE BACK ROW — do not scatter. Crossbow on Edward. Her all-party attack does ~3,000 to Edward and ~1,000 to others. Best Heal user must act every turn. This fight takes several minutes. Win for the true ending — losing gets the second ending.
🏆 Sacnoth — Witness the Sacnoth revelation
🏆 Let's Go Home James! — Win Form 3 and get the true ending
🏆 Dead People Don't Speak — Get the second ending (lose or give up at Form 3)

Weapon & Magic Proficiency
All weapon and magic proficiency achievements are 10pt each. Best farmed during long grinding sessions — equip the weapon or spell you want to level and run battles repeatedly.
KEY ITEMS
Must-Have Items Reference
Pendant — Disc 3: fountain after filling all Icon statues in the underground passage. Required to finish the game — never drop it.
Red Glass Part — Disc 1/2 border: on the floor near the Stained Glass Window in Priest's Quarters.
Brown Glass Part — Drops from Alias. Check inventory after that fight.
Green Glass Part — Disc 2: on the ground after the Roger Bacon / music box scene.
Rope Ladder — Disc 1: open Mira's wardrobe a SECOND TIME after defeating her.
Red Key — Disc 2: dropped by Alias. Opens the second treasure room door and the Keepers room.
Blue Key — Disc 3: on Bessy's body after the guillotine scene.
Green Key — Disc 3: give both dolls back to the children guarding the locked door.
Doll 1 — Disc 2: examine the table during the Keeper's room backtrack (after getting the Red Key).
Doll 2 — Disc 3: examine below the gravestone after Roger unlocks the gate.
Crossbow — Disc 2: picked up from the table in the treasure room. Have it equipped when you reach Alias.
Empty Bottle + Acid — Disc 3: bottle from the cupboard in Patrick's room area; fill it at the Acid Tank near the Mad Fly fight area. Required to make the bomb.
Tindex Box — Disc 3: examine the corpse below the counterweight room. Part of the bomb.
Daniel's Arm — Disc 2: picked up from the floor after the chandelier FMV. Used in Disc 4 to trigger the finale.
