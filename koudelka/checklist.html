🏆 You are Loved! (5pts) — Exorcise Charlotte — give her Sophia's Letters (Option A)
🏆 Charlotte's Wrath (5pts) — Defeat Charlotte boss (Option B)
DECISION POINT: Choose one option before entering Charlotte's Cell
Option A (no boss): Go to Greek code chest in Library — Koudelka knows the code from the glass parts. Get Sophia's Letters — give to Charlotte — receive Flare Brooch — unlocks 'You are Loved!'
Option B (fight Charlotte): Skip the Greek chest — fight Charlotte after the furniture battle — receive Star Brooch — unlocks 'Charlotte's Wrath'. Boss – Charlotte (HP: ~3200) | Medium/Hard | Weak to Fire. Physical (Bow Gun, Mace) best. Fast, inflicts Silence and Poison. Geyser is best magic.

[ Exterminator XIII ] Win 22 consecutive battles in Outer Wall and Bell Tower areas without fleeing
🏆 Exterminator XIII: Bell Tower (5pts) — Win 22 consecutive battles without fleeing
🏆 Vines (3pts) — Defeat Vines — root enemies on the way up
🏆 Patrick's Vine (5pts) — Defeat Patrick's Vine — the corpse-holding root that cannot be killed by magic
🏆 Cat Got My Pendant! (2pts) — Encounter the special black cat and defeat it — appears during the ascent, do not miss
Regular roots: use Flare and Tornado. Roots holding corpses cannot be killed by magic — use Lifedrinker, Crossbow, Gargoyle Claw, or Sacnoth.
Confirm you have the Pendant in your inventory — required to continue past Elaine
🏆 Bested Elaine (10pts) — Defeat Elaine without anyone being knocked out (1st or 2nd encounter) — keep everyone alive
Boss – Elaine Phase 1 (HP: 11,881) | Medium | Physical attacks do nothing. Use magic — Tornado especially effective. One character healing, one fortifying.
Boss – Elaine Phase 2 (HP: 22,801) | Medium | Magic now useless. Switch to physical — Lifedrinker, Sacnoth. Heal and fortify constantly.
🏆 Bested Evolved Elaine (25pts) — Defeat Elaine's final form without anyone being knocked out — keep everyone alive
FINAL BOSS – Elaine Phase 3 | Hard | Pre-fight: all characters HP 4500+, Scroll Items stockpiled, Panaceas ready, level as high as possible. Use magic and strongest weapons (Gargoyle Claw or Sacnoth).
